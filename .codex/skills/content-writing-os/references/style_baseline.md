# Style Baseline

Source anchor:
- https://medium.com/@insyte-from-pranay/calculating-the-cost-of-your-rag-powered-chatbot-693a433f61df

## Audience

Business owners, operators, and decision makers who need to understand AI, LLM, and Agentic AI fundamentals without losing technical accuracy.

## Core Voice

1. Write technical content in clear business language.
2. Explain how systems work, then connect directly to value and risk.
3. Use practical examples, assumptions, and explicit tradeoffs.
4. Keep tone confident, pragmatic, and non-hype.
5. Prioritize clarity over novelty in wording.

## Narrative Pattern

1. Start with business problem and context.
2. Introduce the technical concept with plain-language definition.
3. Explain mechanism step-by-step.
4. Translate mechanism into business impact:
- cost
- speed
- quality
- risk
5. Close with what to do next.

## Non-Negotiables

1. Every factual claim maps to evidence.
2. Distinguish fact vs estimate vs recommendation.
3. Avoid generic motivational language.
4. Avoid unexplained jargon and undefined acronyms.
